FRIDAY NIGHT PARTIES

In 2019, right before the pandemic, my family started the tradition of friday night parties. Every friday night, we would:

-> make our favorite snacks!

-> watch a silly movie (my sister and i always argue about which one)

But after moving to the US, for a while, this is what my friday nights usually look like:

-> lots of homework

-> and trader joe's microwave meals!

I promise though, it isn't as sad as it sounds. It's just a part of the immigrant life. You leave behind old tradition, and try to make new ones. So, here's what my friday nights look like after a year of moving across the globe:

-> boards games with friends; we love poker, spy alley & ticket to ride!

-> eating ice cream together & vibing to 80s rock!

In the end, you make new traditions with your new family and it ends up being okay. Just the cycle of life!
